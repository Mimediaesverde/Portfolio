EQUALITY LOANS

The gender pay gap between men and women is still a latent problem in the world. It is estimated that it would take 105 years to bridge that gap. Bankinter wanted to help reduce that time. This loan that you can pay back in 105 years, aimed at entrepreneurial women and businesses that commit to provide a fair salary among their workers. That way, they’ll have no excuse. We’ll host a landing page where all the necessary information regarding how to access and manage loans will be available, as well as a pay gap meter which we hope will reach zero soon.

BULLYINGPROOF

More than 60% of school students still suffer from bullying. Jockey Plaza wanted to address this situation in the back-to-school season.
Jockey Plaza created a collection that is ideal both for kids and young students and put them “on sale”. These items caused outrage among parents, but also got schools to commit to including methods against bullying in their curricula.
