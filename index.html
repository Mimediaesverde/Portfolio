EQUALITY LOANS


The gender pay gap between men and women is still a latent problem in the world. It is estimated that it would take 105 years to bridge that gap.

Bankinter wanted to help reduce that time. This loan that you can pay back in 105 years, aimed at entrepreneurial women and businesses that commit to provide a fair salary among their workers. That way, they'll have no excuse.

We'll host a landing page where all the necessary information regarding how to access and manage loans will be available, as well as a pay gap meter which we hope will reach zero soon.

BULLYINGPROOF


More than 60% of school students still suffer from bullying. Jockey Plaza wanted to address this situation in the back-to-school season.

Jockey Plaza created a collection that is ideal both for kids and young students and put them “on sale”.

These items caused outrage among parents, but also got schools to commit to including methods against bullying in their curricula.

EQUALITY TIME


In Peru, "La hora Inca Kola" is famous for telling the time between radio commercials.

In a country where cases of femicide are worsening over the years, it was time to make the problem visible in a clearer and more direct way. The hour of equality was a tool to keep telling the time while showing real data about the situation in Peru.

SNIPER TEST


Taking care of our eyes means wearing the appropriate glasses, but many young people refuse to get their eyes tested because they are too lazy to do it or because they think their eyes are just fine, until it is too late.

How can Multiópticas, an eyewear store, make young people get their eyes tested? Playing of course.

We'll create a new firearm, a sniper rifle, with a scope with variable magnification, and we'll link it to some of the most popular shooters like Call of Duty, Fortnite and Counter-Strike. When you download this rifle, you'll try it out in a tutorial with small targets and short missions. When the tutorial is over, you'll see your score and your eye test results, showing you if you might need to visit the eye doctor.

PRESENT WOMEN


#MujeresPresentes is a platform created to make female hard work and talent visible in industries where there still isn't a balanced participation between men and women.

58% of management positions in Banco Falabella are held by women; that's why, on Women's Day, they decided to stress their commitment towards contributing to a society that is more fair and equal.

LET'S BE FREE


Jockey Plaza, the biggest mall in Lima, wanted to deliver a message on Valentine's Day: let's be free to love.

Inside the mall there's an arch that has become iconic and is located in the main square in Jockey Plaza and which will become the center of the campaign.

We'll dress the arch up as a traditional wedding arch and then launch an open invitation to all people who want to be symbolically united with whoever they love, partners, friends, family, pets, anyone.

It is love that matters. We'll start with a content video communicating the importance of freedom to love and on February 14th we will celebrate hundreds of symbolic unions with live music.

THE PEOPLE'S CUT


We know pleasing people is hard, especially when it comes to movie endings or film in general. That's why, in order to promote the next edition of the Festival Internacional de cine fantàstic de Catalunya in Sitges, Barcelona, we'll make an ending that nobody will be able to refuse, because it will be their creation.

30 Monedas is a Spanish horror/fiction series quite popular in the region. Through a series of stories on Instagram, we’ll gradually put together an alternative ending for the series with the fans' assistance.

We'll re-edit the ending following this new version and it will premiere at the festival, where great directors and actors will be able to enjoy it.
